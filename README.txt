#Diary v0.2

This is a personal diary app for personal use. By time, the other languages will be added. (Current language is Turkish.)

The application saves your username and password as you login once.
If you forget any of the information to login, you cannot access your data again.
Every entry you save is encrypts and sends to SQLite database.
You can decrypt them one by one or all of them once.
